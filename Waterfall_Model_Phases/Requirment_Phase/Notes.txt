What is the Requirement Phase?
    The Requirement Phase is the first and most important phase of software development where the needs and expectations of the customer or users are identified, analyzed, and documented.
Purpose of the Requirement Phase
    Understand the problem to be solved
    Identify user needs
    Define system functionality
    Avoid misunderstandings later in development
What is Concept Exploration?
  Concept exploration is the early activity in software development that focuses on understanding what problem needs to be solved and what the software must do, rather than how it will be built.

 It concentrates on requirements, not implementation.
